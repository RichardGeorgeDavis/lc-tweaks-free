# Code of Conduct

This project is intended to be a practical, respectful place for reporting issues, suggesting improvements, and discussing LC Tweaks usage.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, but keep feedback specific and actionable.
- Stay on topic when opening issues or discussions.
- Share enough technical detail for others to reproduce a problem.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, abuse, threats, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or conduct.
- Spam, unrelated promotion, or repeated low-effort posts.
- Publishing private information without permission.

## Reporting

If behavior in this repository becomes inappropriate, contact Lucidity Design:

https://lucidity.design/contact/

Maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, block users, or take other reasonable moderation steps to keep the project useful and safe.
